Welcome to the BEAM Weather Station Dashboard

Explore microclimate data from 40 weather stations across NUS Kent Ridge Campus and UTown, collected under the CoolNUS-BEAM initiative — covering April 2024 to June 2026.

Quick start
  • Click any station on the map to see its temperature, humidity, wind, and solar radiation
  • Use the YEAR buttons (2024 / 2025 / 2026) to switch years — 2024 starts in April, 2026 runs to June
  • ⚖ Compare lets you view up to 8 stations side by side
  • Green circles (G01–G25) are greenery stations measuring air temperature near vegetation

Data is quality-controlled but research-grade; station-years with known sensor issues are flagged on their charts.

Created by Dr. Marcel Ignatius and Joie Lim, College of Design and Engineering, NUS.
CoolNUS-BEAM is funded by the National University of Singapore through the Campus Sustainability initiative, and supported by University Campus Infrastructure (UCI) and the Office of the Deputy President – Research & Technology.
